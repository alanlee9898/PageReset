PageReset Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

PageReset is a Safari extension for macOS that helps make webpages usable again (selection, overlays, clean copy). We designed it to keep your browsing private.

Data we collect

None. PageReset does not collect personal information, browsing history, page content, or analytics.

Local processing

All features run on your Mac inside Safari. Page content is processed locally to restore selection, hide overlays, and format clipboard text. Nothing is uploaded to our servers — we do not operate a backend for this product.

Settings storage

Per-site and global preferences are stored locally using Safari’s extension storage on your device. That data does not leave your Mac through PageReset.

Accounts & tracking

PageReset does not require an account, does not include third-party trackers, and does not use advertising identifiers.

Purchases

If you buy PageReset on the Mac App Store, payment is handled by Apple. We do not receive your payment card details.

Children

PageReset is a general-purpose utility and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date above. Continued use of PageReset after changes means you accept the updated policy.
