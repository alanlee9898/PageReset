PageReset Support

Make any webpage usable again.

Enable the extension

  1. Open the PageReset app once after install.
  2. Click Open Safari Settings… and turn on the PageReset extension.
  3. In Safari, grant PageReset access for the sites you want to clean up.
  4. Use the PageReset button in the Safari toolbar.

Common questions

Nothing happens on a page. Reload the tab after enabling the extension, and confirm Safari has granted access for that site.

Copy shortcuts don’t work. Suggested shortcuts can conflict with other apps. Change them in Safari Settings → Extensions → PageReset.

Overlays aren’t removed. Use Remove overlays from the toolbar, or Zap element for a specific block. PageReset does not remove paywalls, login walls, or paid content.

Selection / right-click still blocked. Those options are off by default. Turn them on for This site or Global in the toolbar popup.
